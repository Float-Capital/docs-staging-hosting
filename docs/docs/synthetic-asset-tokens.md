## Tell me more?

Two types of [synthetic tokens](/docs/faqs#what-is-a-synthetic-asset) are created for each synthetic market, synthetic long and short tokens. Synthetic long tokens benefit from the price appreciation ğŸ“ˆ of the underlying asset. Synthetic short tokens benefit from the price depreciation ğŸ“‰ of the underlying asset.

I.e. Imagine the synthetic market is for Gold. If you buy $100 worth of long Gold tokens, and the price of Gold increase, the value of your token will increase.

These Synthetic Tokens are created dynamically as ERC20 tokens using Open Zeppelin library for ERC20 tokens, whenever a new synthetic market is created.
